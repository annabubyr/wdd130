History

Rapids Whitewater Rafting was founded in 1998 by a small group of passionate river guides. Starting with just one raft and a dream, the founders set out to create safe, exciting, and accessible whitewater experiences for people of all backgrounds.

What began as a handful of guided trips has grown into a trusted rafting company known for unforgettable river journeys, strong safety standards, and a commitment to fun. Today, we proudly welcome adventurers from around the world to experience the thrill of the river.
